# 🛡️ StudentShield AI - Hackathon Demo Guide

This guide is designed to help you deliver a **Gold Tier** presentation. It follows a storytelling narrative that engages judges immediately.

---

## 1. The Problem Statement (The Hook)
**Speech:** 
"Every single day, thousands of students receive an email that looks exactly like this: *'URGENT: Your tuition payment failed. Pay now or be suspended.'* or *'CONGRATULATIONS: You've been selected for a $60/hr Apple Internship.'*

Students are the **#1 target** for cyber-criminals because they are under financial pressure, searching for jobs, and often using new digital tools for the first time. Generic antivirus software doesn't catch these. Regular phishing filters miss the nuance. Students need a specialized shield."

---

## 2. Core Features (The "Wow" Factor)

### A. AI Deep Scanner (Text & Vision)
*   **What it does:** Uses **Gemini 1.5 Flash** to analyze messages.
*   **Unique Value:** It doesn't just look for bad words; it understands the 'Social Engineering' behind the scam. It can even scan **screenshots** of WhatsApp or Telegram chats.

### B. Recruiter Credibility Audit
*   **What it does:** Performs a forensic identity check on recruiters.
*   **Unique Value:** Prevents students from handing over their Resumes and IDs to fake HR accounts.

### C. URL Forensic Engine
*   **What it does:** Analyzes links for character substitution (e.g., `amaz0n.com` vs `amazon.com`) and technical redirects.

### D. Community Threat Intel (Report Wall)
*   **What it does:** A live feed where students can report scams seen on their specific campus.

---

## 3. Demo Walkthrough (The Action)

### Step 1: The "Immediate Danger" Check
1.  Open the **Pre-Scanner**.
2.  **Paste this text:** *"Your University Student ID has been compromised. Log in at http://verify-student-portal.tk to prevent account locking. This must be done within 60 minutes."*
3.  **Show the Result:** Watch as the AI flags the **Sense of Urgency**, the **Suspicious TLD (.tk)**, and the **Phishing attempt**.

### Step 2: The Fake Job Offer (Recruiter Audit)
1.  Go to **Recruiter Audit**.
2.  **Enter:** *"John Smith, Freelance Talent Scout at 'Global Tech Solutions'"*
3.  **Show the Result:** The AI will flag the generic name, lack of verified domain, and suspicious hiring patterns.

### Step 3: The Screenshot Proof
1.  Show how you can **Upload a Screenshot** of a suspicious text message.
2.  The OCR (Optical Character Recognition) extracts the text, and Gemini analyzes the image for visual scam markers (like fake logos).

## 💡 Testing Pro-Tips for Demo:
*   **Use Realistic Data:** When typing in the demo, use actual names of universities or companies the judges know.
*   **Mobile Demo:** Open the app on your phone during the presentation to show the **Mobile Responsiveness**. Show how a student can check a scam while on the bus to class!
*   **Check the Dashboard:** Show the **Security Score** moving up as you perform audits. Judges love "Gamification".
